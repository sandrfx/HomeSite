About

Head of Mobile

Technical leader of the new mobile application for banking (unannounced, oil and gas industry).

My main responsibilities are: designing Mobile First service architecture, organizing agile-teams, building development processes, supporting business from IT point of view to achieve the business goals set by the company.

Loved mobile development since the days of Windows Mobile and J2ME. Started my professional carrier in IT in 2007 as a Java backend developer, and in 2012 moved to Android development.

Since then I have gone all the way from mobile developer to the Head of Mobile and CTO, and have developed different mobile applications for large multinational corporations as well as for startups.
